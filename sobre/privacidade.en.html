Privacy Policy — eskrev

Last updated: February 2, 2026

eskrev is an offline editor. We do not run servers, accounts, or telemetry. This policy describes how data is handled locally on your device.

What information we collect

Information you provide (locally): texts, notes, project names, theme and font preferences, session password, and imported files (.skr).

Local technical information: metadata from the writing process (time, rhythm, insertions, revisions) used only for editor features.

What we do not collect: we do not capture location, contacts, images, audio, browsing history, advertising identifiers, tracking cookies, or account data.

How we use your information

  • Keep the editor working offline in your browser.
  • Save your content and preferences on the device.
  • Allow local export, import, and technical verification.

Where your information is stored

All data stays on your device, using browser local storage (localStorage, IndexedDB, and Cache Storage). We do not send data to servers.

Your rights and choices

Because data is local, you can:

  • Export your texts and projects whenever you want.
  • Delete the session and clear local data.
  • Remove files and notes directly in the editor.

Under Brazil’s LGPD, you have rights of access, correction, deletion, and portability. In eskrev, those rights are exercised locally on the device.

The security of your information

We use technical measures to keep processing local. Security depends on your device and browser. We recommend exporting and keeping periodic backups.

How long we keep your information

Until you delete the session or clear browser data. You are in control.

Information related to teenagers

eskrev is not directed at children under 13. If you are a legal guardian and need guidance, contact us.

Privacy Policy updates

We may update this document. The update date will be shown at the top.

Contact

If you have privacy questions, write to: oi@rafa.pro.br.

Additional terms — Brazil

In compliance with LGPD, you may request information and exercise your rights. Since eskrev does not collect server data, requests are handled locally within the app through export and deletion of data on the device.
